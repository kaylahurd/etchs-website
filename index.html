About ETC

Pioneers in Occupational Health Technology

Environmental Technology Corporation Hearing Solutions (ETC Testing) is a company dedicated to providing quality medical testing, equipment, and services to industry nationwide.

We were instrumental in the research and development of the first microprocessor-based audiometer - unveiled at the National Safety Congress in Chicago - and have continued setting standards in occupational health ever since.

CAOHC Certified Staff

All technicians complete training meeting ASHA, AIHA, AIMA, AAIN and AAOM standards, approved by the Council for Accreditation in Occupational Hearing Conservation.

Audiologist-Validated Reports

Every audiometric test is validated by an audiologist with at minimum a Master's Degree, ensuring professional-grade analysis.
